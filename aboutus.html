ABOUT US & OUR WORK

Who We Are -This Is Palestine

Organization

This Is Palestine is an organization dedicated to providing aid to those who are suffering in Palestine as atrocities continue to be commited against the Palestinian people.Beyond being an organization, I am an impassioned individual deeply committed to advocating for the rights and liberation of Palestine.This organization is rooted in the rich cultural heritage and patriotic spirit of Palestine.

Mission Statement

This Is Palestine is a global collective committed to alleviating the suffering in Palestine. Beyond aid, we amplify the voices of the oppressed, advocating for their rights and liberation.Our mission is to bring hope to those facing atrocities,illuminating the path to resilience and justice.

Our Work : What We Do

Donations

Through the sales of our products, we are able to donate profits to our partners and organizations that are on the ground in Palestine, who directly provide aid to Palestinian civilians with a focus on Gaza.Aid includes food, water, shelter and many more essentials.Our products are made by Palestinian families who work very hard to create and deliver high quality products, which further helps represent Palestine.

Representation

The products sold are more than just items; they symbolize unwavering support for the Palestinian struggle for justice, freedom, and rights. The range includes Kuffiya scarves, clothing, jewelry, flags, accessories, and posters.

News

This Is Palestine uses its social media channels to deliver daily news from the ground in Palestine, especially Gaza, countering false narratives and providing accurate information even at the risk of censorship.

How We Do It

Aid Delivery

Donations are directed to partners like Islamic Relief USA (IRUSA) who have longstanding presence in Palestine. These organizations purchase from local businesses and provide direct aid to civilians, thus supporting the local economy.

News Channels

Information is sourced from journalists, news channels, and civilians in Palestine, ensuring the most accurate and up-to-date reporting.

Why We Do It

Philanthropy and Gratitude

Having experienced oppression firsthand, the team feels obligated to help those currently suffering in Palestine. Their work is driven by a sense of gratitude and a desire to aid those in dire need.

Representation

The products represent the enduring spirit of the Palestinian people, contributing to their livelihood and showcasing their resilience to the world.
