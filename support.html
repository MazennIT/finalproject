Where Are Your Products Made?

Our products are sourced from various regions including Palestine, and we collaborate with local artisans and suppliers to ensure quality and authenticity.

What Is Your Return Policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. Items must be in their original condition and packaging. Custom items and final sale products are not eligible for returns.

How Can I Track My Order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number. You can use this number on our website's tracking page to monitor your order's status.

Do You Ship Internationally?

Yes, we ship to most countries around the world. Shipping costs and delivery times vary based on the destination.

What Payment Methods Do You Accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, PayPal, and other secure payment options.

Can I Change or Cancel My Order?

If you need to change or cancel your order, please contact our customer service as soon as possible. Once an order has been processed, changes or cancellations may not be possible.

How Do I Contact Customer Service?

You can contact our customer service through the contact form on our website, or by emailing us directly at info@thisispalestine.co. Our team is available to assist you with any questions or concerns.
